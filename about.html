Welcome to TechMotive, your premier destination for hassle-free and reliable car rentals. At TechMotive, we understand that your journey is as important as your destination. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to providing a seamless and enjoyable experience for every traveler.


Meet the Partners

Ron Ancheta - Driving Innovation

Ron Ancheta, co-founder and visionary behind TechMotive, brings decades of experience in the automotive and technology sectors. His passion for innovation and commitment to customer satisfaction drive the company's mission to revolutionize the car rental industry. Ron believes in leveraging technology to enhance the overall rental experience, making it more efficient and user-friendly.

Kevin Carriedo - Customer-Centric Approach

As the co-founder of TechMotive, Kevin Carriedo is the driving force behind our customer-centric philosophy. With a background in customer service and operations, Kevin ensures that every aspect of our service is tailored to meet your unique needs. His dedication to excellence and personalization has earned TechMotive a reputation for putting customers first in the car rental industry.

Lee Romero - Sustainable Mobility Advocate

Lee Romero, co-founder of TechMotive, is passionate about sustainable mobility solutions. With a deep understanding of environmental impact and a commitment to corporate social responsibility, Lee strives to integrate eco-friendly practices into our operations. Under his guidance, TechMotive is working towards a future where car rentals align with sustainable practices, contributing to a greener planet.


Our Story

TechMotive started as a shared vision among three friends who shared a passion for both technology and automobiles. Recognizing the need for a modern, customer-centric car rental service, Ron, Kevin, and Lee came together to create TechMotive. Since our inception, we've been on a mission to redefine the car rental experience by combining cutting-edge technology, exceptional customer service, and a commitment to sustainability.

At TechMotive, we don't just provide cars; we offer a journey tailored to your preferences. Whether you're a business traveler, a family on vacation, or an adventure seeker, TechMotive is here to make your travel experience memorable, convenient, and enjoyable.

Thank you for choosing TechMotive. Let's drive into the future together.
